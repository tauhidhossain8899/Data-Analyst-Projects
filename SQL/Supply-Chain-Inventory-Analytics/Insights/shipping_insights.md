# Shipping & Logistics Analysis Insights

## Overview

This analysis evaluates transportation modes, shipping carriers, and delivery routes to identify cost-efficient and high-performance logistics operations.

---

## Key Findings

### 1. Air Transportation Has the Highest Shipping Cost

Air transportation recorded the highest average shipping cost of **6.02**, making it the most expensive transportation method.

### 2. Sea Transportation Is the Most Cost-Efficient Mode

Sea transportation recorded the lowest average shipping cost of **4.97**, making it the most economical transportation option.

### 3. Carrier C Has the Highest Shipping Cost

Carrier C recorded the highest average shipping cost of **5.60**, indicating higher logistics expenses compared to other carriers.

### 4. Carrier B Is the Fastest Shipping Carrier

Carrier B achieved the lowest average shipping time of **5.30 days**, making it the fastest delivery provider.

### 5. Carrier B Provides the Best Overall Performance

Carrier B offers both:

* The lowest average shipping cost (**5.51**)
* The fastest average shipping time (**5.30 days**)

This makes Carrier B the most efficient carrier in the supply chain network.

### 6. Route C Is the Fastest Route

Route C recorded the shortest average shipping time of **5.25 days**, outperforming Route A and Route B.

---

## Business Insights

* Air transportation provides speed but increases operational costs.
* Sea transportation is suitable for cost-sensitive shipments.
* Carrier B demonstrates the best balance between cost and delivery speed.
* Route C appears to be the most efficient logistics route.
* Carrier C should be reviewed to determine whether its higher costs are justified.

---

## Recommendations

### Recommendation 1

Increase shipment allocation to Carrier B due to its superior cost and delivery performance.

### Recommendation 2

Use Air transportation only for urgent or high-priority shipments.

### Recommendation 3

Prioritize Route C for time-sensitive deliveries.

### Recommendation 4

Review Carrier C's pricing and performance to identify optimization opportunities.

### Recommendation 5

Implement a logistics strategy that balances shipping cost and delivery speed to improve overall supply chain efficiency.

---

## Conclusion

The shipping analysis identified Carrier B and Route C as the most efficient logistics options. Optimizing carrier selection and transportation methods can reduce operational costs while maintaining strong delivery performance.
