# Revenue Analysis Insights

## Key Findings

1. Total revenue generated across the supply chain was $577,604.82.

2. Skincare products generated the highest revenue ($241,628.16).

3. Skincare contributed approximately 41.84% of total revenue.

4. SKU51 was the highest revenue-generating product ($9,866.47).

5. Cosmetics products generated the highest average revenue per product ($6,212.36).

## Recommendations

- Increase investment in the skincare category because it drives the largest share of revenue.

- Investigate the success factors behind SKU51 and apply similar strategies to other products.

- Explore opportunities to expand cosmetics products since they generate the highest average revenue.
