# Inventory Analysis Insights

## Key Findings

1. SKU68 has the lowest stock level and may face stockout risk.

2. SKU12, SKU59, and SKU51 maintain the highest stock levels.

3. Cosmetics products have the highest average stock level (58.65 units).

4. Haircare products contribute the largest total inventory volume (1644 units).

## Recommendations

- Monitor SKU68 closely and consider replenishment.
- Review inventory levels of SKU12, SKU59, and SKU51 for possible overstocking.
- Investigate why cosmetics maintain higher average stock levels.
- Optimize inventory allocation across product categories.
