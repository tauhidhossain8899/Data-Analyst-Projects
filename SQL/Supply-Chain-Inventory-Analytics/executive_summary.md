# Executive Summary

## Project Overview

This project analyzes supply chain and inventory performance using SQL. The objective is to identify business insights related to inventory management, revenue generation, supplier performance, and logistics efficiency.

## Key Findings

- Total revenue generated was $577,604.82.
- Skincare generated the highest total revenue ($241,628.16).
- SKU51 was the highest revenue-generating product.
- Haircare maintained the highest total inventory (1,644 units).
- Supplier 1 generated the highest revenue and had the lowest defect rate.
- Carrier B achieved the fastest shipping time while maintaining competitive costs.
- Route C was the most efficient delivery route.

## Business Recommendations

1. Increase focus on skincare products due to strong revenue performance.
2. Monitor SKU68 because of low stock levels.
3. Strengthen relationships with Supplier 1.
4. Review Supplier 4 for cost optimization.
5. Increase shipment allocation to Carrier B.
6. Prioritize Route C for faster deliveries.

## Conclusion

The analysis demonstrates how SQL can be used to transform operational data into actionable business insights for supply chain decision-making.
